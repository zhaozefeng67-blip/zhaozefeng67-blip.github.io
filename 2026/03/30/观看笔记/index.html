当我把美式零元购带到中国家电展

I have a problem.

My parents expectations（/ˌekspekˈteɪʃn/） of China are too high

There are countless vedios where foreigners（注意，有弱读） are blown away by China’s high tech advancements and when my parents came to China for the first time, they got to see it with their own eyes.

Awesome, right?

But here’s the problem

they see those vedios online and think that high technology is everywhere

the other day,

my mom messaged（读成了 message） me on wechat and asked（读成了 asked）me if a robot or drone brings my delivery up to my apartment door.

just because when（原视频并没有读出来） she was in China, a robot brought her delivery to the door and she also saw drone delivery on the great wall

so it must be everywhere.

huh, maybe all that high tech stuff just hasn’t made it to our normal lives yet.

or maybe I’m wrong?

so I came to the AWE exhibition this time to see if I can find I some amazing new home technology to impress them.

when they come back at the end of the month, maybe I can get them some unique Chinese tech souvenirs for them to bring back with them

OK, let’s head in.

I’ve noticed an interesting phenomenon.

many products are heavily emphasizing specs（参数） and concepts.

that’s pretty cool.

But as a consumer, I couldn’t help wondering: what should technology ultimately serve?

everyone can see how technology is developing today.

but I believe technological development and innovation are all aimed at making intelligence serve our lives better.

we all hope to find products that are smarter, easier to use, and in turn（可以理解成进而） make our lives easier.

I think Gree’s ZhenAi might just be the answer that I’m looking for.

how exactly does technology love us?

the key home appliances to check out are the essential trio（三件套） that everyone needs. 这句话优点绕，核心意思是，接下来我们要重点看的家具是“必备三件套”

refrigerator, washing machine, air conditioner

you can say that your satisfaction with these three home appliances directly determines how comfortable you daily life is

In the US, I’m used to doing a big grocery haul（超市大采购） once a week.

So a large refrigerator is an absolute most for me.

this fridge has pretty slim body

so how big is its actual capacity?

this 508 liter super freeze series show dragon fridge（508 升的超冻系列雪龙） has the capacity to store all kinds of familiy food.

I mean just look how big it is.

the drinks are even cold.

I really like its 3D dual system cooling（3D 双系统制冷） which keeps ingredients fresh without mixing odors.

plus its（这里有连读） light oxygen moisturing freshness technology（轻氧水润焕鲜技术） keeps vegetables fresh for up to a week and crisp in your fridge.

how about that?

pretty nice right?

this is really good for someone like me who loves to stock up on food.

this part of me is still pretty American.

what can I say（可以翻译成 没办法）

I just really really love buying

also I really like drinking ice drinks.

so the ice cubes tend to take up all of the space in my fridge

so ever since I’ve come to China, I’ve really really been wanting a big fridge（冷藏） and freezer（冷冻）

just like this

I think even my parents would want one like this hey

maybe I’ll save these for when they come

they can have some for themselves

so really nice souvenirs they’re giving out, aren’t they

Got to (do something)： 这里的 got to 不是“必须”，而是 “有机会、终于能够” 。它暗示这种经历是难得的、令人兴奋的。

With their own eyes： 这是一种 强调修辞（Pleonasm）。虽然看肯定是用眼睛看，但加上 own eyes 就像中文里说“亲手接手”或“亲耳听到”一样，增加了真实性和不可反驳性。

见到偶像/奇观：
“I’ve seen photos of the Aurora, but I never thought I’d get to see it with my own eyes.”
（我看过极光的照片，但我从没想过能亲眼见到它。）

证实一个难以置信的传闻：
“People said the tech was revolutionary, and today they got to see it with their own eyes.”
（人们都说这技术是革命性的，今天他们终于亲眼见证了。）

blown away

The other day 是一个非常高频且好用的口语表达，它的中文意思非常接近于：

前几天

Example: “I had a weird dream the other night.”

为什么这里用 up to，而不是 to？

如果博主只说 to my door，那只是在交代“目的地”。

但加上了 up，就带上了一种**“深度”和“突破感”**。

普通情况 (To)： 外卖员把东西送到公寓（Apartment）。

震撼情况 (Up to)： 机器人不仅进了大楼，还上了电梯，穿过走廊，一路“顶”到了我的房门口。

形象理解：

想象你在玩闯关游戏，原本以为机器人只能在大门口止步，结果它**“更进一步”**，直接挪到了你卧室门外。这个“向上/向内”延伸的过程，就是 up to。

Head in 和 get in

Head in 更自然，get in 更强调费力

“Optimizing the code reduces latency, which in turn improves user experience.”
（优化代码能降低延迟，从而提升用户体验。）

this part of me 这个表达在英语里很常见，用来承认自己某一面的特质，通常带点自嘲或坦然的语气：

  • This part of me will never change. （我这一面永远不会变）

  • This part of me is still a little kid. （我内心这部分还是个小孩）

和 Kerry 说的一样，很口语，很自然，有一种自我审视的感觉。
